Her Bedtime Stories

Sensitive content warning: Trauma, Sexual Coercion
Her Bedtime Stories tells the story of giving in to thoughts related to trauma, through the visual device of a platform that is constantly sinking deeper into a mine. Your role is to observe and help when you can. This game was created in 72 hours for the Ludum Dare game jam, but also in dedication to Sexual Assault Awareness Month(SAAM), April 2021.
