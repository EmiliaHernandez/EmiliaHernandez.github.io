UCF STUDENT GOVERNMENT

studentgovernment.ucf.edu

Through employment in the UCF design group, student government is one of four webpages that are maintained, updated, and improved upon by myself and my teammate. The initiative tracker was built by myself last year, containing all the plans that the administration has for student life. Student government places the most orders, often with leadership changes, documentation replacements, and ... The intiative tracker previously displayed the full description of the initiative, and it was requested to change it to a responsive drop-down. This is the live product, which constantly changes as initiatives are completed.

Technology used: Wordpress, Github, Bootstrap, Php

UCF Office of Student Involvement

osi.ucf.edu

The office of student involvement's website, again is managed my myself and my teammate. The preview displays a webpage that I developed solo, from a design request made by the student director of the agency being represented. In the design groups, orders are placed for web changes, and sometimes there is complete creative freedom, and other times there are requirements, such as with this design! It was originally split into two pages: home, DJ roster, and events. This was consolidated into one page and this is the finished project.

Technology used: Wordpress, Github, Bootstrap, Php, Adobe Illustrator, Figma

UCF Pegasus Palooza

palooza.sdes.ucf.edu

UCF's official welcome week has a micro-site, which was completely redesigned by the design group web team. My specific contributions to the site was the illustrations on the Home, About, and Events pages. Not only that, but my teammate and myself created Lo-fi and Hi-fi mockups of the sites using Figma. After having worked in the design group for a year, I famliarized myself with the creative limits I was allowed while still following the brand guide that was given to me. The project took 1 month to complete, and was successfully launched in June, about two months before the event. A side-by-side comparison of this project's redesign can be found here

Technology used: Wordpress, Github, Bootstrap, Php, Adobe Illustrator, Figma
