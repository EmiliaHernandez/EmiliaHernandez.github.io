Cultural Center Mural

Cultural Center Mural

Student Goverment and the Multicultural Student Center held a university-wide call for submissions for a student art contest. The winning design was printed as a mural and is on display in the UCF Student Union. This piece was a collaboration between myself and a graphic design alumni. My specific contributions were the lettering and imagery within the letters.

Storm Drain Sign

Storm Drain Sign

I entered the UCF Arbortetum Storm Drain Design Contest and won. The contest encouraged the use of mascots, and because UCF has both the pegasus(collegiate) and Knightro(athletic), I submitted two entries that featured each of them. Having experience working in the office of student involvement, I was familiar with the UCF brand and kept to the established colors, fonts, and symbols. The storm drain signs are projected to be printed and displayed throughout the campus during the Fall 2022 semester
