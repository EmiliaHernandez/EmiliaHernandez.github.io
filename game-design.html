Her bedtime stories is a visual novel about a recovering victim of.... is playable in-browser via itch.io on desktop

Her bedtime stories was created in 72-hours for the ludum Dare game jame competition. The theme was "deeper and deeper", and my interpretation of that was that it may become harder to get out of a hole the deeper you are, mentally. However, I did want this game toend positively and included a reminder to keep those close to you around for support and strength during hard times.
